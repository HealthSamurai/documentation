# App Gallery

The App Gallery is a public website where patients can discover and launch approved SMART on FHIR applications.

## Overview

Patients can:

- **Browse Apps** — View all approved healthcare applications
- **Launch Apps** — Connect apps to their health data

## Browse Apps

The gallery displays all active apps in a grid layout.

### Filtering

Use filters to find apps by:

- **Platform** — Web, iOS, Android
- **Category** — Health management, clinical apps, etc.

### App Details

Click on any app to see:

- Description and features
- Privacy policy and terms of service
- Supported platforms
- Developer information

## Launch an App

1. Click **Launch** on the app you want to use
2. Sign in with your patient credentials
3. Review the permissions the app is requesting
4. Click **Allow** to grant access

The app will open with access to your health data based on the permissions you approved.

### Revoking Access

If you no longer want an app to access your data, you can disconnect it from your account settings.
